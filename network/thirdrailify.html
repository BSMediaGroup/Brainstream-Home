ThirdRailify

Site Overview

ThirdRailify is a daily podcast led by @ThirdRailify (Shawn), a stay-at-home father delivering independent, personality-driven commentary on news, crime, and pop culture. The platform pairs candid perspective with a professional presentation and includes a merch store.

Programming Focus

  • Daily episodes centered on current events, crime coverage, and pop culture discussion.
  • Creator-led perspective grounded in family life and direct, independent commentary.
  • Audience engagement supported by clear brand identity and an on-site merch store.
